[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="0fc90-104">Klikněte na **Správa servisu** \> **Obecné** \> **Servisní smlouvy** \> **Servisní smlouvy** .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fc90-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements** .</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fc90-105">Vyberte servisní smlouvu, ze které chcete odstranit servisní kusovník (BOM).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fc90-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>
