[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

1. <span data-ttu-id="844be-104">Přejděte na Řízení projektu a účetnictví > Projekty > Všechny projekty.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-104">Go to Project management and accounting > Projects > All projects.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="844be-106">Vyberte ID projektu, který se používá pro dobropis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-106">Select the Project ID that is used for the Credit Note.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="844be-107">Klikněte na odkaz na vybraném řádku v seznamu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-107">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="844be-109">Klepněte na Deníky faktury.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-109">Click Invoice journals.</span></span> 
6. <span data-ttu-id="844be-110">Vyberte transakci pro dobropis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-110">Select the transaction for credit note.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="844be-111">Označte v seznamu vybraný řádek.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-111">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span>

8. <span data-ttu-id="844be-113">Klikněte na Výběr pro dobropis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-113">Click Select for credit note.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="844be-114">Zaškrtnutí políčka vyberte transakci pro dobropis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-114">Select the transaction for credit note by marking the check box.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="844be-115">Klikněte na tlačítko OK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-115">Click OK.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="844be-116">Po provedení tohoto kroku bude k dispozici dobropis pro návrh faktury.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="844be-116">After this step, the Credit Note will be available for Invoice Proposal.</span></span>
